Pizza

Description

This is a discus Italian dish

Ingredient

  • Pizza Dough
  • whole peeled tomatoes
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • fresh mozzarella
  • 1 cup basil leaves

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 450F
  2. roll out pizza dough into a large circle
  3. prepare the tomato sauce:
    • hand-crush tomatoes
    • add garlic, salt, pepper
  4. Top the dough with the sauce and add cheese
  5. Bake 20-25 minutes
