Lasagna

Description

This is a layered Italian dish.

Ingredient

  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Butter
  • Ground Beef
  • Lasagna Noodles

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 350F
  2. cook ground beef
  3. alternate layering
    • 1/4 Cup sauce
    • beef
    • cheese
    • noodle sheet
  4. Top layer is always cheese
  5. Bake 40 minutes
