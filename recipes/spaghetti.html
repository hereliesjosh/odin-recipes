Spaghetti

Description

This is a noodle Italian dish.

Ingredient

  • Sugar
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Ground Beef
  • Spaghetti Noodle

Steps

  1. Boil noodles for 9 minutes
  2. cook ground beef
  3. Add sauce and stir in 2 Tbsp. Sugar
  4. Cook medium until heated through
  5. Add parmesan cheese
